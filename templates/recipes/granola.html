How to make granola

{% endblock header %} {% block content %}

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of oats
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 cup diced nuts
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 250 deg F
  2. Mix all of the ingredients in a big bowl. Use whatever nuts you want.
  3. Spread the mixture onto a baking tray.
  4. Bake for 100 minutes (1 hour, 40 minutes), occationally mixing the granola
